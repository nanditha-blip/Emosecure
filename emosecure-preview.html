How It Works

1. Sense — Biometric signals (HR, skin conductance, stress, facial tension) are collected in real time.

2. Classify — An AI model detects the emotional state: Calm → Anxious → Distressed → Crisis.

3. Adapt — Encryption level upgrades automatically: AES-128 → AES-256 → RSA-4096.

4. Protect — Psychological records are secured with the appropriate algorithm and access tier.
